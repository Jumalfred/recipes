RECIPE FOR FRIED EGG

Home Fish Pilau

Ingredients
  • 100ml of cooking oil
  • 2 eggs
  • Table salt
  • 2 sliced tomatoes
  • Freshly grounded pepper

PROCEDURE
  1. In a pan of medium heat,heat cooking oil for few seconds.
  2. Crack egg,add salt and stir then pour it into the pan.
  3. Cook for some minutes until white is just set
  4. Add the sliced tomatoes into the pan and continue cooking.
  5. Remove from the pan and season it with pepper.

SERVING

Serve with strong tea(for breakfast).................
