RECIPE FOR FISH

Ingredients
  • 8 ounce tomato sauce
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • 3-4 cups of water
  • 3 pounds(1.4kg) white fish cut up in steaks
  • Â¼ cup(60ml) cooking oil
  • salt

PROCEDURE
  1. Wash the fish carefully, drain, and pat dry with paper towels.
  2. Heat the oil in the medium saucepan; add the onion ,wait for about a minute.
  3. Pour tomato sauce, white pepper and bouillon powder. Stir for about a minute and let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes. Add about 3 cups of water season with salt according to preference and stir .
  4. Add fish steaks and white pepper one at a time. Simmer until the fish is flaky and cooked through, about 10 minutes. Adjust thickness of soup with water , salt and more spices

SERVING

Serve when hot with white rice...................
